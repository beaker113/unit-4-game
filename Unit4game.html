Crystal Collector!

At the start of the game you will be given a random number.

Random numbers will also be assigned to the four crystals below, however this value will be hidden from you.

To win the game you must match your score to the chosen number by clicking on the crystals. If your score goes over the chosen number, you lose.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
